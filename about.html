About Us

Our History

St.Joyuna Academy was started in the year 2002 by Eunice Mboya on a strong christian foundation. The school is located in Ruai, Kamunyonge village.The school has been able to grow up to a student population of 500 pupils. This growth has been driven by the holistic stature and nurturing of the pupils who end up doing very well in life. We provide holistic education that will equip the pupils with Christian values, Knowledge and skills for the development of society.

Mission

Nurturing the potential of every learner holistically through studying and building capacity.

Vision

Empowering children with quality education and erradicating illiteracy within and beyond our community.

Core Values

  1. Godliness
  2. Discipline
  3. Respect
  4. Patriotism
  5. Integrity
  6. Responsibility
  7. Peace,Love and Unity
