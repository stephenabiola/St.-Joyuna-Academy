Academics

Our Academic Courses

We place great importance on personal development, academic achievement, the arts and sporting success. We are a school where the individual flourishes and contributes to the overall success of the team.

CBC PlayGroup

Children will mostly learn through play.They also learn to socialize through play. They learn to share and build relationships with each other and adults.

CBC PP1 and PP2

Children will mostly learn from activities other than theory. They will also be engaged in digital literacy and they cover the content in the following subjects.

CBC Grade 1, 2 and 3

After two years in pre-primary, learners will join lower primary. This will include grade 1, grade 2, and grade 3. The minimum age to join grade one is 6 years.

CBC Grade 4, 5 and 6

At this level,
learners will have a broad curriculum and they will be given an opportunity for experimentation & exploration.

CBC Grade 7, 8 and 9

Junior secondary will expose the students to a wide-based curriculum to allow them to discover their own capabilities, character, and potential.

Co-Curricular

Co-curricular activities are at the heart of our curriculum. At St. Joyuna academy we offer a wide range of sporting activities and every pupil is encouraged to participate in atleast one of the sports
We intend that the experience gained through sports will develop the skills and attitudes necessary for involvement in an active and healthy life.

School Football Team

At St. Joyuna we nurture the future footballers through St.Joyuna Football Academy.

Swimming Club

At St. Joyuna we nurture the future swimmers through St.Joyuna Swimming Club.

Drama Club

At St. Joyuna we nurture the future comedians and artists through St.Joyuna Drama club.
