PP2 Graduation

PP1 and PP2 are early levels of education in the new curriculum in Kenya.

The PP2 graduation marks a major milestone for children and their
parents as they watch their children transition to the next phase of
their academic life.

It is the joy of every guardian to see their children honing and developing
their talents, skills and potentials in becoming better citizens.

Games and Sports

The Sports and Games Department is responsible for the provision of recreational and competitive sports for both students and the members of staff in the school. The services in the Department of Sports and Games are a concerted effort of the technical (Games tutors and Coaches) and the teaching staff.

Sports and Games Department offers an array of disciplines namely: rugby, basketball, handball, netball, athletics, swimming, cross country, and tug of war.

Student Leadership

Providing students with leadership experience gives them the ability to collaborate, communicate, problem-solve, build relationships, think critically and outside the box, by allowing them to learn to adapt to changing and challenging situations.

This helps by preparing them for the world outside of the classroom.
