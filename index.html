Our School

A decade's worth of rich history and goodwill with the public schools surrounding us plus an extensive community consultation process prepared us for growth

Ruai, Kamunyonge Village

St. Joyuna Academy was established in 2002 with an appropriate population of 1 pupil. Over the years the institution has grown by leaps and bounds and opened its doors to not only admitting Kenyan students but also international students. At St. Joyuna, it’s our intention that our pupils receive all the things one can expect from a primary education: the chance to explore and discover new things about the world and themselves, the possibility to realize their potential, attain their goals and develop skills knowledge and attitude necessary for lifelong learning

The schools started as a one building institution – currently the Pre-school Block. In 2022, we constructed our Upper Primary block.

At St. Joyuna we;

  • Provide a caring, cooperative and safe environment.
  • Welcome parents and community members as part of learning family.
  • Uphold high standards of teaching and learning.
  • Respond to unique qualities of individual students.
